This page explains what data huynhdu.info collects and how it's used. Responsible party: Huỳnh Dự (huynhdu@huynhdu.info).

## Data collected

**Newsletter signup.** If you enter your email in the subscribe form, it's sent to [Buttondown](https://buttondown.com) — a third-party newsletter provider — to store and send new post notifications. Signup uses double opt-in: you must confirm via email before you actually start receiving anything.

Besides the newsletter form, this site has no other form collecting personal information.

## Cookies & analytics

- **Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics (GA4)**: only starts running after you click **Accept** on the cookie banner shown when you visit. If you click **Decline** or don't interact, these tags never load.
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics**: measures traffic without cookies, without localStorage, and without collecting personally identifiable information, per Cloudflare's own disclosure — so it doesn't require separate consent.
- **Cloudflare Turnstile**: runs on the newsletter form to block bots, without showing real users a manual captcha. See Cloudflare's [Turnstile Privacy Notice](https://www.cloudflare.com/turnstile-privacy-policy/).

## Comments

If the comment feature (Remark42, self-hosted) is enabled, the name and email you enter when commenting are stored on that system to display comments — this feature is **not active yet** on the site.

## Your rights

You can request to view, correct, or delete personal data related to you (e.g. unsubscribe from the newsletter, remove your email from the list). Use the **"Request to delete your data"** link in the footer, or email huynhdu@huynhdu.info directly.

This policy follows the spirit of Vietnam's Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP on personal data protection. Content may be updated as the site adds new features.
