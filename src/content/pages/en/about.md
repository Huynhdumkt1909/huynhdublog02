Hi, I'm **Huỳnh Dự** (full name Huỳnh Văn Dự, also written Huynh Van Du). I'm a hybrid marketing, event production, and business operations professional based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, with **15+ years of experience** delivering brand growth, large-scale event execution, exhibition activation, and channel engagement across construction materials, entertainment, and lifestyle businesses.

I'm proud and grateful to work in a field I genuinely love. It lets me keep connecting with talented people, collaborating with great teams, and constantly learning something new — from complex things like training AI tools, to simple things like figuring out how many hinges a door actually needs.

## What I do now

I'm currently **Head of Marketing at Aseanwindow** (since Nov 2022), leading a 10-person team across brand campaigns, communications, and exhibition execution. The project I'm proudest of is the **["Largest Sliding Aluminum-Glass Door in Vietnam"](/blog/bo-cua-lon-nhat-viet-nam)** campaign (write-up currently in Vietnamese, English version coming) — certified by VietKings, covered by 20+ media outlets, and tied to a charity donation of 400 million VND.

## Career highlights

- **Head of Marketing — Aseanwindow** (2022 – present): repositioned the Xingfa brand from affordable to premium; led the record-breaking door campaign; ran nearly 100 nationwide technical workshops that grew sales by 300%; managed 2,000m²+ of exhibition booths at Vietbuild with integrated AR/VR/MR experiences.
- **Marketing Manager — Anh Duy Group** (2016 – 2022): grew market presence for distributed audio brands (Klipsch, Denon, JBL, Accuphase...); produced **"Gọi Tên Bốn Mùa,"** a free tribute concert for musician Trịnh Công Sơn at Hoa Lư Stadium — nearly 20,000 attendees on-site, 3M+ online views.
- **Marketing Manager — SaigonDance** (2014 – 2021): built the studio's overall digital marketing strategy, and worked as Production Executive for the Vietnam Belly Dance Festival — one of Vietnam's first nationally visible belly dance competitions.

## Skills & tools

Brand marketing, large-scale event production, budget and vendor management, PR and media relations, B2B channel training, digital transformation (LarkSuite, Google Workspace, automation with n8n), plus hands-on content production (Premiere Pro, After Effects, 3D with Blender/SketchUp, AR/VR experiences with Meta Quest).
